What is RockSat-X @ VT?

RockSat-X

RockSat-X is an annual collaboration project between undergraduate students at various universities across the United States and NASA. Each university team creates an individual payload to perform scientific experiments in low earth orbit. RockSat-X @ Virginia Tech has a history tht spans multiple years, and includes projects like recording videos for virtual reality simulations and designing and testing one of the first 3-D printers in space. To see news about our RockSat-X program, check out our facebook page.
